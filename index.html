About Me

As of August 2025, I am working in the Theory of Atoms research group of Prof. Ludmil Katzarkov at Institute of Mathematics and Informatics at the Bulgarian Academy of Sciences, Sofia, Bulgaria.

My research interests include birational geometry of singular Fano varieties and computer algebra.
